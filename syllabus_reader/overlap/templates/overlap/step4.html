Objective

Evaluate overlap scores by clicking on the canvas. You can click up to 12 times to test different positions.

How it works
  • The canvas shows a greyed-out evaluation area
  • Click anywhere to test overlap with the opposing team's circle
  • Red points will appear showing your click positions
  • Each click calculates an overlap score based on the opponent's circle from Step 3
  • Results are recorded in the table below
Scoring

Overlap scores range from 0 (no overlap) to 100 (maximum overlap) based on how much your click position would overlap with the opponent's circle.
